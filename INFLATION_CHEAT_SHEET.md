# DTFBL Auction Inflation Cheat Sheet

## The Formula

```
INFLATION = ($ Left in League) ÷ (Spots Left) ÷ 18.5
```

## Step-by-Step Calculator Entry

```
[Total $ remaining] ÷ [Spots remaining] ÷ 18.5 =
```

**Example:** $1400 left, 60 spots left
```
1400 ÷ 60 ÷ 18.5 = 1.26
```
That's 26% inflation - multiply your values by 1.26

## What the Numbers Mean

| Inflation | What's Happening | Your Move |
|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| **< 0.95** | Deflation - money tight | Be patient, get bargains |
| **0.95 - 1.05** | Normal | Stick to your values |
| **1.05 - 1.15** | Mild inflation | Pay 5-15% over value |
| **1.15 - 1.30** | Heavy inflation | Pay 15-30% over value |
| **> 1.30** | Crazy inflation | Let others overpay, wait it out |

## Your Adjusted Bid Formula

```
Adjusted Bid = (Player Value) × (Inflation)
```

**Example:** Corbin Carroll, value $25, inflation 1.20
```
25 × 1.20 = $30 max bid
```
